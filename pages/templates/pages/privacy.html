Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Introduction & Scope

ANITINN ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information when you register, visit, purchase from, or sell on the ANITINN Marketplace. It applies system-wide across all buyer and vendor interfaces.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to facilitate sales and maintain site security:

  • Account Details: Names, email addresses, password hashes, and phone numbers.
  • Vendor Profile: Business registration licenses, Tax ID (TIN), payout MoMo numbers, or banking details.
  • Analytics: IP addresses, device types, browser categories, and behavior pathways to power our AI recommendation models.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your details are processed to secure your marketplace presence:

  • Processing orders, shipping products, and routing financial settlements.
  • Powering the AI-Powered Copywriting assistant for vendor products.
  • Improving inventory optimization models, analytics trends, and search ranking displays.
  • Notifying users regarding account verification, stock levels, or order status logs.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell or lease customer or vendor listings to commercial data miners. Data is shared with verified shipping fleets to facilitate delivery, and mobile money/banking services to complete payout settlements securely.

5. Data Security & Encryption

ANITINN employs modern 256-bit AES encryption structures and SSL certificates. Payout parameters are sandboxed securely. However, no data transmitting channel is 100% immune, and we advise users to maintain solid passwords.
